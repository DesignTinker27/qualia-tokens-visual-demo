üéØ Quick Decision Guide

\n
    \n
  • Inside a component? Use space_04 to space_16
    • \n
  • Between components? Use space_16 to space_32
    • \n
  • Between page sections? Use space_48 to space_96
    • \n
  • Unsure? Start with space_16 - it works for most situations
    • \n
